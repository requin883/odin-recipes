Pizza Recipe

Description:

This is a pizza with pepperoni recipe, basically tomato sauce, cheese and lots of pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. In a small bowl add water and salt
  2. Mix the water with the flour until the dough is consistent, after that, let the dough rest on a dry place.
  3. On a round plate expand the dough in a circular shape pressing the edges onto the plate
  4. Peel the tomatoes and blend them to get the tomato sauce
  5. Add on top of the circular-shape dough the tomato sauce, cheese, and the pepperoni(Leave a small space between the dough edges and the sauce)
  6. Put the pizza in the oven for 30-40 minutes (390deg celcius) or until the crust has a golden color
